Welcome to Amit's Sukkot project!
your go-to destination for discovering and exploring the world of movies.
This website is designed to provide a seamless and enjoyable experience for movie enthusiasts of all ages.

Features
Search and Filter: Easily find movies by title or explore our curated collections.
Save Favorites: Create your personal watchlist and never miss a movie.
Read Descriptions: Dive deeper into the the movie's data.

How it Works
The website utilizes the TMDB API to access a vast database of movies and their information. By searching for a movie or browsing our categories, you can discover new titles and add them to your favorites.
